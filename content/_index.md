# Clément Vidon

Trained at ECAL in Lausanne and La Cambre in Brussels, I spent five years in Paris shooting fashion, design, and architecture. Exploring digital **art** led me to combine **photography** with programming and pursue computer science at 42 in Paris, where I developed a strong interest in **software** and automation. Today, I split my time between portrait and documentary photography, interdisciplinary research, product development, and DevOps.
